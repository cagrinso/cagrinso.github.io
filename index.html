Graduate Student in Mathematics

My research interests include algebraic geometry, finite fields, almost perfect nonlinear functions, finite geometry, cryptography, combinatorics, and cohomology theory. I am particularly interested in geometric structures arising from combinatorial objects and the geometry behind the factorization of multivariate polynomials.

About Me

I am a Ph.D. student working at the intersection of algebraic geometry, topology, and combinatorics.

Research

Exceptional Almost Perfect Nonlinear Conjecture

Studying multivariate symmetric polynomials and their factorizations through the geometric structure of the singularities of their leading forms.

Geometry and Combinatorics

Exploring relaxed Kähler package structures on loop spaces and their connections to combinatorial problems.
